What country generates the most electricity from nuclear power?

  1. Japan
  2. United States
  3. France
  4. Russia

INCORRECT CORRECT

Answer is the United States.

The United States has the most nuclear capacity and generation among the 31 countries in the world that have commercial nuclear power. France, the country with the second most nuclear capacity, relies on nuclear power for nearly 80% of its electricity. Other countries that get a significant share of their electricity from nuclear power include Russia, Japan, South Korea, and Germany.

Japan's nuclear power generation was significantly curtailed in the aftermath of the March 11, 2011 Tohoku earthquake and accompanying tsunami, which led to the accident at Tokyo Electric Power Company's (TEPCO) Fukushima Daiichi nuclear power plant and subsequent outages at other plants.
