Which is the largest hydroelectric facility in the United States?

  1. Grand Coulee Dam, Washington
  2. Hoover Dam, Nevada
  3. Hells Canyon Dam, Idaho
  4. Niagara Falls, New York

INCORRECT CORRECT

Answer is Grand Coulee Dam, Washington.

The 7,079-megawatt Grand Coulee facility, located on the Columbia River, is the largest hydroelectric plant in the United States. It is one of the top 10 electricity producers in the world.

Source: U.S. Bureau of Reclamation (Public Domain)
