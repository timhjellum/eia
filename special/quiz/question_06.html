Biomass, produced from plant-based materials, provides about half of all renewable energy consumed in the United States. What type of biomass energy do we use most?

  1. Biofuels (ethanol and biodiesel)
  2. Trash
  3. Landfill gas
  4. Wood

INCORRECT CORRECT

Answer is Wood.

Did you know that before 1875, the United States relied on wood as its primary source of energy? Today, wood and wood waste (bark, sawdust, wood chips, wood scrap, and paper mill residues) provide about 2% of our energy use. Many manufacturing plants in the wood and paper products industry use wood waste to produce their own steam and electricity.
