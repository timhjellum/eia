What energy source provides the most renewable energy in the United States?

  1. Biofuels
  2. Wind
  3. Hydropower
  4. Wood

>Previous question Next question>




INCORRECT CORRECT

Answer is hydropower.

Americans used renewable energy sources to meet about 9% of our total energy needs in 2011. Hydropower provided the most energy, followed by wood, biofuels, wind, biomass waste, geothermal, and solar. Hydropower accounted for 35% of our renewable energy use and 63% of electricity generation from renewables in 2011.
