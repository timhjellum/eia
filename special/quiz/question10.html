What country generates the greatest share of its electricity from wind power?

  1. Denmark
  2. Ireland
  3. Spain
  4. United States

Next question >

< Previous question

INCORRECT CORRECT

Answer is Denmark.

About 20% of Denmark's electricity generation came from wind in 2010. The next highest shares of wind generation are found in Portugal (17%), Spain (15%), Ireland (10%), and Germany (6%). While the United States generates more wind-powered electricity than any other country, wind provided only about 3% of our Nation's electricity in 2011.
