How many gallons of gasoline are refined from one 42-gallon barrel of crude oil?

  1. 9
  2. 19
  3. 29
  4. 42

>Previous question Next question>




INCORRECT CORRECT

Answer is 19.

One 42-gallon barrel of crude oil, when refined, produces about 19 gallons of finished motor gasoline, 11 gallons of diesel, and 15 gallons of other petroleum products. But, how can one 42-gallon barrel of crude oil yield over 44 gallons of petroleum products? This gain from processing crude oil is similar to what happens to popcorn, which gets bigger after it's popped.
