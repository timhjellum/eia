Reusable Content Containers

The hide-mt class is used to hide empty list items. You can learn more about this functionality by visiting the States > Empty List-items.

There's only two types of row class names, l-row and l-row header. The difference it that the default row adds a 22px space on the left and sometimes has a 22px space on the right (based on the type of content). As a developer or content editor, always use the l-row as the other is reserved for special applications.

Any list can have a footer by adding a :class="foot" to the li or list-item. The foot class will automatically add a space and arrow, ›, after the link.

Code sample on the online style guide. 
<!-- List footer -->
<li class="foot"><a href="somelink.html">Description</a></li>
